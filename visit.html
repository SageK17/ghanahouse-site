Hoe te bereiken How to get here

Met de autoBy car

Betaald parkeren rondom de Korte Haaksbergerstraat. Parkeergarage Van Heekgarage op 5 minuten loopafstand.

Paid parking around Korte Haaksbergerstraat. Van Heek garage is a 5-minute walk away.

Met OVBy transit

Centraal Station Enschede op 8 minuten lopen. Vanaf Station Enschede West ook te belopen.

Central Station Enschede is an 8-minute walk. Also walkable from Station Enschede West.

Bike parking around the building. Guarded storage at CS Enschede.
